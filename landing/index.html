What it does

Point your camera at a Ghanaian meal — our hybrid AI (Ghana-trained model + Gemini) identifies dishes like Banku, Jollof, and Waakye, then shows nutrition estimates per serving.

Meal scan

Health coach
Steps, macros, weather-aware safety tips, and dietitian advice.

How it works

  1. Open the app and point your camera at a meal.
  2. The model detects the dish and shows nutrition.
  3. Save or upload optional logs to your account.
